Welcome to Innovation Hub, Purulia

The Innovation Hub at the District Science Centre in Purulia, West Bengal is a dynamic initiative by the National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), Ministry of Culture, Government of India. Established to foster creativity and scientific curiosity among youth, the hub offers a range of facilities designed to nurture innovative thinking.

Key Features of the Innovation Hub

  • Discovery Hall: Features 10 to 15 interactive science exhibits and experiments, encouraging visitors to explore scientific principles through hands-on activities.
  • Innovation Resource Centre & Hall of Fame: Showcases transformative innovations and inventions, highlighting their impact on society and the stories behind them.
  • Idea Lab: Equipped with basic facilities for model making, science experimentation, and the design and fabrication of practical gadgets to support creative projects.
  • Design Studio: Provides a creative environment for designing various objects and products, fostering design thinking among participants.

Specialized Areas in the Innovation Hub

  • 🔧 Thod Phod Jod (Break & Remake) Corner: Encourages students to dismantle, reassemble, and remake devices and gadgets, promoting practical learning.
  • ♻️ Kabad Se Jugad (Build from Scraps): Allows students to create projects using everyday scrap materials, enhancing resourcefulness and sustainability.
  • 💡 Idea Box: A space where students can generate and store innovative ideas, with the best ones selected for further experimentation and development.

2023
Weather Station
Our weather station uses BMP680, PM2.5, PM10, LTR-390, and a CO sensor to monitor real-time local weather conditions. Equipped with an interactive interface, it provides live environmental data. This innovative model won 1st prize at the State Level Eastern India Science and Engineering Festival at BITM Kolkata in 2023.
