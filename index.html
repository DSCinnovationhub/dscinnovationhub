Welcome to Innovation Hub, Purulia

The Innovation Hub at the District Science Centre in Purulia, West Bengal is a dynamic initiative by the National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), Ministry of Culture, Government of India. Established to foster creativity and scientific curiosity among youth, the hub offers a range of facilities designed to nurture innovative thinking.

Discovery Hall

Features 10 to 15 interactive science exhibits and experiments, encouraging visitors to explore scientific principles through hands-on activities.

Innovation Resource Centre

Showcases transformative innovations and inventions, highlighting their impact on society and the stories behind them.

Idea Lab

Equipped with basic facilities for model making, science experimentation, and the design and fabrication of practical gadgets to support creative projects.

Design Studio

Provides a creative environment for designing various objects and products, fostering design thinking among participants.

Specialized Areas in the Innovation Hub

Thod Fod Jod (Break & Remake) Corner

Encourages students to dismantle, reassemble, and remake devices and gadgets, promoting practical learning.

Kabad Se Jugad (Build from Scraps)

Allows students to create projects using everyday scrap materials, enhancing resourcefulness and sustainability.

Idea Box

A space where students can generate and store innovative ideas, with the best ones selected for further experimentation and development.

Our Achievements

Dec 19, 2024
Smog Hawk
Debraj Roy, Shubham Mahato & Ujan Rajak
Developed an innovative drone-based pollution monitoring system, which effectively utilises drone technology to collect real-time data on environmental pollution levels. This project earned 2nd place at Techfest 2024, organised by IIT Bombay, Mumbai, and was awarded a cash prize of ₹20,000.
Nov 12, 2024
H.A.R.I.S.H.
Arijit Rakshit
We are developing an advanced Hypervision AI named 'Harish.' We have also applied for copyright protection for this innovative AI technology.
March 12, 2024
Weather Station
Shubham Mahato & Subhradeep Karmakar
Our weather station uses BMP680, PM2.5, PM10, LTR-390, and a CO sensor to monitor real-time local weather conditions. Equipped with an interactive interface, it provides live environmental data. This innovative model won 1st prize at the State Level Eastern India Science and Engineering Festival at BITM Kolkata in 2023.
January 09, 2025
Arduino-based Garbage Cleaner
Prattay Jha
Developed an Arduino-based Garbage Cleaner designed to effectively clean waste from ponds and rivers. This innovative model secured 1st place at the District-Level Science Fair and achieved 3rd position at the State-Level Science Fair 2025.
January 09, 2024
X-Air
Debraj Roy, Shubham Mahato
Got 1st Prize in Innovation Festival 2024 Organised by Digha Science Centre & National Science Camp
